DT-670 silicon diodes.

Specifications

Standard curve Curve 10
 
Recommended excitation 10 A 0.1%
 
Max reverse voltage 40 V
 
Max current before damage 1 mA continuous or 100 mA pulsed
 
Dissipation at recommended excitation
17W at 4.2 K; 10 W at 77 K; 5 W at 305 K
 
Thermal response time SD: typical <10 ms at 4.2 K; 100 ms at 77 K, 200 ms at 305 K
 
Use in radiation Recommended for use only in low level radiation for more information click here
 
Use in magnetic field
Not recommended for use in magnetic field applications below 60 K. Low magnetic field dependence when used in fields up to 5 tesla above 60 K for more information click here
 
Reproducibility1 10 mK at 4.2 K
 
1 Short-term reproducibility data is obtained by subjecting sensor to repeated thermal shocks from 305 K to 4.2 K
